# Brainstorming - Idea Generation - Prioritization Template
## 1. Introduction
Brainstorming is a structured creativity technique used to generate innovative ideas and solutions for a given problem. In the context of the FitHub CRM (Fitness Center Management using Salesforce) project, brainstorming plays a critical role in identifying challenges faced by fitness centers and proposing effective digital solutions.
This phase focuses on:
- Encouraging team collaboration 
- Generating multiple ideas 
- Evaluating and prioritizing the best solution 
The goal is to move from a problem space to a solution-oriented approach using systematic thinking.

## Step 1: Team Collaboration & Problem Identification
### Team Collaboration
The project team conducted a collaborative session to identify real-world issues in fitness center operations. Each member contributed ideas based on observations, research, and user experience.
### Identified Problems
The following key issues were identified:
- Manual membership tracking leads to errors 
- Lack of automated payment reminders 
- Inefficient trainer allocation 
- Poor class scheduling system 
- No centralized data management 
### Final Problem Selection
After discussion, the team selected the primary problem:
“Lack of an integrated system to manage fitness center operations efficiently.”
## Step 2: Idea Generation & Grouping
### Idea Generation
The team generated multiple ideas without filtering to encourage creativity.
###   Idea	                       Description
Fitness Mobile App      	Tracks workouts and progress
Trainer Booking System	    Allows trainer scheduling
Payment Reminder System	    Sends alerts for dues
AI Fitness Assistant	    Suggests workouts
CRM System (FitHub)	       Complete management solution

## Idea Grouping
The generated ideas were categorized into:
- Operational Solutions
(CRM System, Trainer Management) 
- User Experience Solutions
(Mobile App, AI Assistant) 
- Financial Solutions
(Payment Reminder System) 

## Step 3: Idea Prioritization
### Evaluation Criteria
Each idea was evaluated based on:
- Feasibility 
- Scalability 
- Impact 
- Implementation Complexity 

### Prioritization Table
### Idea	       Feasibility	  Impact	Scalability	 Final Score
Mobile App	     Medium	      Medium	    High	   7
AI Assistant	  Low	      High	       Medium	   6
CRM System	      High	      High	       High	       9
Reminder System	  High	      Medium	   High	       8

### Final Selected Idea
FitHub CRM (Salesforce-Based Fitness Center Management System)
This solution was selected because it:
- Solves multiple problems in one system 
- Is scalable using Salesforce platform 
- Improves operational efficiency 

## Conclusion
The brainstorming phase successfully transformed raw ideas into a structured solution. The selection of FitHub CRM ensures that the system is:
- Efficient 
- Scalable 
- User-friendly 
This phase lays the foundation for further requirement analysis and system design.
