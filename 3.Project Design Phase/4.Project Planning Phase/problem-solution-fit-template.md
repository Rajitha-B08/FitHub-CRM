# Problem Solution Fit Template

## 1. Introduction

The Problem-Solution Fit Template is a structured framework used to ensure that the proposed solution effectively addresses the identified customer problems. It helps in aligning customer needs, behaviors, and constraints with the designed solution.

For the FitHub CRM (Salesforce-based Fitness Center Management System), this template validates how well the solution meets the requirements of fitness centers, trainers, and members.

---

## 2. Customer Segments

Customer segments represent the target users of the system.

### Primary Customers

- Gym/Fitness Center Owners (Admins)
- Fitness Trainers
- Gym Members

### Secondary Customers

- New users interested in fitness services
- Management staff

---

## 3. Jobs-To-Be-Done (Problems)

These represent the key tasks or problems customers need to solve.

- Manage member registrations and records
- Assign trainers efficiently
- Schedule classes without conflicts
- Track payments and subscriptions
- Communicate effectively with members

---

## 4. Available Solutions

Current solutions used by customers include:

- Manual registers and notebooks
- Excel spreadsheets
- Basic mobile apps (limited features)

### Limitations of Existing Solutions

- Time-consuming
- Error-prone
- Lack of automation
- No centralized system

---

## 5. Customer Constraints

Constraints that limit customers from adopting solutions:

- Limited budget for advanced software
- Lack of technical expertise
- Dependence on manual processes
- Limited internet access in some cases

---

## 6. Customer Behavior

### Online Behavior

- Searching for fitness apps
- Booking sessions online
- Receiving notifications via mobile

### Offline Behavior

- Visiting gym physically
- Manual payments
- Direct interaction with trainers

---

## 7. Triggers

Triggers are events that motivate customers to take action:

- Membership expiry reminders
- New class announcements
- Fitness goals or health concerns
- Peer influence (friends joining gym)

---

## 8. Emotions (Before & After Solution)

### Before Using FitHub CRM

- Confusion in scheduling
- Frustration due to missed payments
- Lack of organization

### After Using FitHub CRM

- Confidence in system
- Better control over operations
- Improved user satisfaction

---

## 9. Channels of Interaction

### Online Channels

- Web application (Salesforce UI)
- Email notifications
- Mobile access

### Offline Channels

- Gym front desk
- Direct trainer interaction

---

## 10. Problem Root Cause

The main reasons behind the problem include:

- Lack of digital transformation
- Dependence on manual systems
- Absence of integrated platforms
- Poor data management practices

---

## 11. Proposed Solution

### FitHub CRM (Salesforce-Based Solution)

The system provides:

- Centralized data management
- Automated workflows
- Real-time updates
- Efficient trainer allocation

It aligns with customer needs by:

- Reducing manual effort
- Improving communication
- Enhancing operational efficiency

---

## 12. Conclusion

The Problem-Solution Fit analysis confirms that FitHub CRM effectively addresses the core challenges faced by fitness centers. The solution aligns well with customer needs, behaviors, and constraints, ensuring high adoption and usability.

This validation supports moving forward to detailed system design and implementation.
